Blockchain Lab at College of William and Mary

Known as the backbone of many crypto-currencies such as BitCoin, Blockchain technology, the new distributed ledger technology, is believed to revolutionize many aspects of the world. A blockchain serves as a trusted platform to record verifiable transactions without a central authority, which could lower transaction costs and improve efficiency. For example, supply chain industry is gaining edges by providing a transparent system to track all assets and transactions. Internet communities are using blockchain to provide currency incentive to stimulate participation. The ledger is shared publicly and records anything from ID, biometric information, to credit history, to education record, almost anything information. As a result, top financial institutions and many companies in industry are jumping on the bandwagon experimenting the new technology.
